What is Stellar Hacks?
Working with the Child Rights and You Foundation (Cry), Stellar Hacks strives to enhance the lives of underprivileged children in India through the power of coding. We aim to unite the bright coders and thinkers of our future generation while also helping children in rural areas attain the resources to achieve their full potential.
